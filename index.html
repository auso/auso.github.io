Adolfo Martínez Usó


Welcome to my website

I am a post-doc researcher in the Departamento de Sistemas Informáticos y Computación (DSIC) of the Technical University of Valencia (UPV), Spain. I belong to the DMIP research group, being also enrolled in the REFRAME project. I have been working at the Institute of New Imaging Technologies (iNIT) in the computer vision research group from 2002 to December, 2013.





Research interest

Predictive models for multidimensional data when the resolution context changes.

Semi-supervised/active learning.

Image Segmentation (using colour images and multi/hyperspectral images).

Interaction Systems using mobile phone data.

Signal and video processing by means of working on Silhouette Extraction in human action video sequences.

Teaching

No teaching at this moment.
